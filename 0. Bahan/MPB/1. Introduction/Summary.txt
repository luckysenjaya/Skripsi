Ringkasan Kuliah
Tujuan:

Memahami Proses Bisnis
Memahami Manajemen Proses Bisnis
Mampu Memodelkan Proses Bisnis menggunakan BPMN
Mampu Menerapkan Siklus Manajemen Proses Bisnis
Mampu Mengotomasi Proses Bisnis menggunakan BPMS
Ringkasan Materi:

Definisi proses bisnis, manajemen proses bisnis (BPM, BPI), system informasi dan perannya dalam menentukan keberhasilan suatu organisasi.
Business Process Management (BPM) Life Cycle
Pemodelan proses bisnis (block diagram, ANSI flow chart, functional flow chart, geographical flow chart;  Business Process Modeling and Notation (BPMN); Penggunaan tools pemodelan BPMN)
Analisis proses bisnis eksisting (as-is model) 
Disain proses bisnis (to-be model)
Otomasi proses bisnis menggukanan BPMS/ Work Flow system Camunda berbasis Java 2 Enterprise Edition (J2EE).
Referensi:

Fundamentals of Business Process Management, Springer, 2013
Business Process Improvement, McGraw-Hill, Inc. 1991
BPMN 2.0 (www.bpmn.org)
Camunda BPMS (www.camunda.org)
BPMN 2.0 & Process Implementation Examples https://github.com/camunda/camunda-bpm-examples#getting-started-with-camunda-bpm
